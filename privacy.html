Privacy Policy

Last updated June 24, 2026

William Covey Media, LLC ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you use our website and how we use it.

What we collect

When you submit our booking or contact form, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, brokerage, phone number, email, property address, and shoot details — so we can respond and schedule your shoot. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

How we use it

  • To contact you about your inquiry and schedule and deliver your media.
  • To send invoices and project communications.
  • To improve our services.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers that help us operate (for example, our form and email providers), and only as needed to deliver our services. We may disclose information if required by law.

Cookies & analytics

This site uses minimal cookies. If analytics are enabled, they help us understand site traffic in aggregate; they do not identify you personally.

Your photos & licensing

Media we create for a paid project is licensed to you for your real estate marketing. We may display non-confidential work in our portfolio and on social media unless you ask us not to.
