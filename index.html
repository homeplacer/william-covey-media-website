Good questions

Booking, prep, and the things agents always ask.

How do I book?

Right from this site — call 843.957.6915 or use the contact form below. We confirm the shoot date and address, then you (or the seller) make sure the home is photo-ready when we arrive.

When will my photos and video be ready?

Final images and video are delivered by email within 48 hours of the shoot. Most shoots come back the next morning.

How does payment work?

Pay after the shoot. An invoice arrives with your media — once it's paid, you have full access to download and use everything in your marketing.

What if the weather turns?

We'll reschedule. Same-day cancellations by the agent are charged at 100%; weather reschedules are no charge.

What's your cancellation policy?

24-hour notice for any changes. We wait up to 30 minutes at the property. Same-day cancellations are billed at 100% of the booked services.

Do you cover my area?

The full Grand Strand — Myrtle Beach, North Myrtle Beach, Surfside, Murrells Inlet, Pawleys Island, Conway, and surrounding towns. If you're not sure, just ask.

How should the home be prepped?

Lights on, blinds open, clutter off counters, vehicles moved off the driveway, pets out of frame. We'll send a full prep checklist when we confirm the shoot.
